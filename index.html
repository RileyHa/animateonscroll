Journey to The Moon

The story of a girl who always admired the moon

Fifi's small pleasure

Fifi always loved gazing up at the sky. What she liked the most was the moon that changed its shape from time to time. She would spend hours looking at the moon.

Airplane and the Moon

One day, Fifi spotted an incredible sight: the airplane was passing right next to the moon. She thought to herself, "I want to be on the plane and touch the moon."

On the Plane

Fifi convinced her parents for almost a month, and she finally got a chance to be on the plane. Just thinking how she can look at the moon in a close distance made her heart bounce like crazy.

Disappointment & New Dream

After few hours, Fifi could see the moon on top of the tall building. However, she was disappointed to realize that she could not touch the moon. Thus, she thought to herself: "I want to be someone who can touch the moon."

Mission Accomplished -1

This is how Fifi became an astronaut. Right now, she is on her first journey of her long-desired dream, Touching the Moon.

Mission Accomplished -2

At first, she was merely thrilled to look at unbelievable scene of the countless stars that embroidered the sky. But as she was approaching the moon, her heart bounced like crazy, like the first time she was on the plane.

Mission Accomplished -3

"3, 2, 1, Ready to Land." Fifi took her first step on the moon. Tears of joy covered her eyes. The moon looked more beautiful up close.
